Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to 2PartsCut. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

2. Information We Don't Collect

2PartsCut is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to emphasize that:

  • We do not collect any personal information from you.
  • We do not upload your video files to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser.
  • We do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies.
  • We do not store any information about the videos you process.
  • We do not have access to the content of your videos.

3. How Our Application Works

2PartsCut is a fully client-side web application, which means:

  • All video processing happens directly in your browser using WebAssembly technology (ffmpeg.wasm).
  • Your video files never leave your device.
  • The split video parts are generated locally and can be downloaded directly to your device.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers during the video processing.

4. Browser Storage

We use your browser's temporary storage capabilities only for the purpose of processing your videos. This data is automatically cleared when you close the application tab or window.

5. Third-Party Libraries

We use the following third-party libraries to provide our service:

  • ffmpeg.wasm - For video processing
  • JSZip - For creating ZIP archives
  • Toast.js - For notifications

These libraries operate entirely within your browser and do not transmit any data externally.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is intended for use by all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special provisions for children's privacy.

7. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
